Frequently Asked Questions

What types of pastries do you sell?

We offer various types of pastries for you customers to enjoy! these include churros, cookies, cake, croissants and so much more!

Business Hours?

We are open at 7-10pm Mondays-Sundays.

What's different in your pastries?

Each pastry at Harana is a story waiting to be shared. Our bakers draw inspiration for creating a diverse menu that caters to different tastes and preferences.

Do you accept custom pastries for occasions?

Yes we do! here at Harana we make sure that your occasions will always be special!

Do you offer gluta-free or vegan menu?

While we are mostly using traditional ingredients, we are actively exploring for gluta free and vegan alternatives! stay tuned!

Are you available for delivery or pickup?

Yes you can place an order for delivery to enjoy your pastries at the comfort of your homepage or place a pickup and skip lines of waiting!

Is the food clean and safe?

At Harana, we prioritize the cleanliness and safety of our food. We adhere to strict hygiene standards in the preparation, handling, and storage of our ingredients. Our kitchen is regularly sanitized, and we follow rigorous protocols to maintain a clean and safe environment.

Is the staff friendly?

At Harana, our staff is dedicated to providing a warm and welcoming experience. You can expect friendly and genuine interactions, whether you're a regular customer or a first-time visitor. You are always welcome!

Does the store allow pets?

Yes, at Harana, we welcome well-behaved pets in our pet-friendly establishment. We understand that pets are part of the family, and we want you to enjoy our pastries with your furry companions!

Do you offer beverages?

While we do take pride for our pastries we also offer beverages! a cup of coffee to start your display or maybe a relaxing sweet afternoon tea? we got you covered!
